HOW DOES SECURE.PAYGH Escrow Work?

1. SECURE.PAYGH reduces the risk of fraud by acting as a trusted third-party that collects,
holds and only disburses funds when both Buyers, Brokers and Sellers are satisfied.

2. Buyer and Seller agree to terms - Either the Buyer or Seller begins a transaction. After registering at SECURE.PAYGH,
all parties agree to the terms of the transaction.

3. Buyer pays SECURE.PAYGH- The Buyer submits a payment by approved payment method to our secure Escrow Account, SECURE.PAYGH verifies the payment,
the Seller is notified that funds have been secured 'In Escrow'.

4. Seller delivers merchandise to Buyer - Upon payment verification, the Seller is authorised to send the merchandise.
SECURE.PAYGH verifies that the Buyer receives the merchandise.

5. Buyer accepts merchandise - The Buyer has a set number of days to inspect the merchandise and the option to accept or reject it.

6. If the Buyer accepts the merchandise - SECURE.PAYGH pays the Seller by releasing the funds to the Seller from the Escrow Account.




Transaction is complete - safely and securely!
